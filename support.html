Pondr FAQs

What is Pondr?

Pondr is a platform for curious people to create, consume and share bite-sized shorticles on any topic of your choice. It consists of eloquent short stories on diversified subjects with sources for endless learnings. Our belief is that sharing of ideas and experiences moves humanity forward. We want to connect the people who have knowledge to the people who need it, to bring together people with different perspectives so they can understand each other better, and to empower everyone to share their knowledge for the benefit of the rest of the world.

How do I start using Pondr?

Pondr works by having the community read and write bite-sized shorticles on the platform. Use your feed to tell us what you're interested in reading. Your feed is filled with stories that interest you. You can view your feed by going to the homepage. Build a good feed by following topics and people, and taking actions in feed that tell us what you like.

  • Topics: When you follow a topic, shorticles with that topic will appear in your feed
  • People: When you follow people, you will see shorticles posted by these people in your feed.
  • The easiest first step: start by following some topics that you care about and some people who write about them.
  • Like and Comment: When you read a post that you like, make sure to encourage the writer by liking and/or commenting on their post to show your support.

How do I add/Change my profile picture?

You can add or change your profile picture:

  • Visit your profile page, which you can access via the person icon on the navigation tab.
  • Tap the "• • •" icon
  • Tap on the option to "Edit Profile"
  • Tap on "edit"

How do I start writing on Pondr?

In order to start writing on Pondr, you can:

  • Tap on the ‘+’ button the navigation tab.
  • Add your title by filling up the ‘Your Title Here’
  • You can start writing your post by filling up the ‘Write something wonderful’ section.
  • You can add a cover photo by clicking on the ‘Cover Photo’ icon at the end of your screen.
  • Similarly, you can add relevant links and sources by clicking on the ‘Source’ icon.
  • You can use the ‘Topics’ section to categorize your post under a particular topic.

How can I follow people on Pondr?

To follow people on Pondr,

  • Tap on the Writer’s Name to visit their profile
  • You can follow the writer by tapping on the yellow ‘follow’ icon.

How do I share Pondr shorticles that I like on other websites?

In order to share Pondr shorticles on other websites, you can tap on the arrow up icon at the right-end of your screen. You will be able to post the shorticles on multiple mobile apps and websites as per your need.

How to Edit or Delete published shorticle?

  • Go to profile by tapping on person icon on navigation tab.
  • Long press or tap and hold your finger on shorticle which you would like to edit or delete.
  • A menu will appear at bottom of the screen.
  • Tap "Edit" to edit the shorticle, or "Delete" to delete the art

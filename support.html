Support / FAQ

What is Pondr?

Pondr is a platform for reading and writing bite-sized articles that fits your mobile screen.

Why should I use Pondr?

For people who like to read, Pondr is a great way to discover new content and information about topics they care about. Articles take just 45 seconds to read and hence you can consume content on the go. It is great for modern smartphone users for consuming interesting content on the go.

Who can read and write on Pondr?

Currently writing on Pondr is under private beta and we are open for people to join our beta program. We will stop taking new admissions once we reach 1000 users with access to write. We are trying to get like-minded people who are interested in business, startups, technology, personal growth and entrepreneurship to write on our platform.

Reading on Pondr is accessible to all.

What can I write about?

You can write about anything interesting and intriguing. However, content related to personal growth, entrepreneurship, startups, technology will get more distribution at this point.

Who sees my content?

Your content is public and anyone with the app can see all the content you publish.

How is my content distributed?

Currently, we are testing out multiple algorithms to figure out the most optimum way for content to reach relevant user. As we test it, for a limited time, the content is distributed chronologically, that is newest first. Our initial users can make the most of it while this is the case as they will get instant distribution on the homepage as soon as they publish the articles.

How can I edit/delete content on Pondr?

Go to profile → Long press on an article you want to edit or delete → Select from the dialog that opens.

Why can't I follow people?

We are still under beta, and the option to follow people will be made available once we have a certain critical mass of users on our platform.

How to report an article?

Tap on article, a modal with Report option will appear.

How can I contact support?

You can contact us directly by emailing us at team@getpondr.com . Your feedback is highly valuable to us.
